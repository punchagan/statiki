  • I do not wish to use the web-service. What do I do?

    Statiki provides a utility script to let you do yourself, what Statiki does for you. Download the script, install the requirements, and run!

  • How do I use a static site generator other than Nikola?

    Start with the manual steps described above, and then modify the .travis.yml and travis_build_n_deploy.sh to suit your needs.

    In future, statiki may allow users to choose a static site generator to use. You are more than welcome to contribute this feature!

  • I setup my demo site. How do I make a new post?

    Statiki has setup a Nikola based site for you.

    To create a new post, you will need to create a new .rst file in the posts/ directory. Copy the metadata from the sample file, 1.rst and modify it, to suit your needs.

    For more information, see Nikola handbook

  • RSS feeds or Galleries don't work properly

    Yes, Statiki uses a stable version of Nikola, and these are known issues.

  • How do I prevent a change from getting published?

    You can ask Travis CI not to build a specific commit by adding [skip ci] or [ci skip] in your commit message.

  • I wish to use a custom domain for my site. How? do not wish to use the web-service. What do I do?

    You can ask GitHub to serve from your domain by simply adding a CNAME file with the name of your domain to the files/ directory of your repository. For more information, see here.

  • How do I stop Travis CI from spamming me?

    I wouldn't recommend completely disabling emails, but see here if you wish to completely disable emails or tweak related settings.
